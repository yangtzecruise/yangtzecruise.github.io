Yichang’s Best Hotels for Yangtze Cruise Passengers

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Yichang, the gateway to the majestic Three Gorges, is a must-visit destination for travelers embarking on a Yangtze River cruise. Whether you’re staying pre- or post-cruise, choosing the right hotel can make or break your trip. From luxury riverside resorts to budget-friendly stays with easy access to the port, here’s a curated list of Yichang’s best hotels tailored for cruise passengers.

Luxury Stays for an Unforgettable Experience

1. The Three Gorges Hotel

Overlooking the Yangtze River, this 5-star gem combines modern elegance with breathtaking views. Guests rave about the spacious rooms, top-notch service, and proximity to the cruise terminal. The hotel’s Chinese and Western restaurants cater to all palates, while the spa offers post-cruise relaxation.

2. Yichang Marriott Hotel

A favorite among international travelers, the Marriott boasts sleek interiors, a rooftop bar, and a prime downtown location. Its 24-hour concierge ensures seamless transfers to the pier, and the executive lounge is perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

Mid-Range Comfort with Convenience

3. Ramada Plaza Yichang

Situated near the Yichang East Railway Station, this hotel is ideal for those arriving by train. The indoor pool and fitness center are great perks, and the shuttle service to the cruise port eliminates hassle.

4. Yichang International Hotel

A blend of affordability and style, this hotel features panoramic city views and a renowned Sichuan restaurant. The free Wi-Fi and business center make it a hit with digital nomads.

Budget-Friendly Options Near the Port

5. Hanting Express Yichang Cruise Terminal

For no-frills comfort, Hanting Express delivers clean rooms and walking-distance access to the port. The 24-hour front desk is a lifesaver for late-night arrivals.

6. GreenTree Inn Yichang Three Gorges Dam Hotel

Budget travelers love this chain for its reliable service and free breakfast. It’s a short taxi ride from the dam and cruise terminals.

Unique Stays with Local Flair

7. The Xiling Gorge Resort

Nestled in the hills, this resort offers tranquil gardens and traditional architecture. It’s a bit farther from the port but worth it for the authentic Yichang vibe.

8. Yichang Wanda Realm Hotel

Attached to a shopping mall, this hotel is perfect for last-minute souvenirs. The kids’ club and family rooms cater to multi-generational travelers.

Pro Tips for Cruise Passengers

  • Book Early: Hotels near the port fill up fast during peak cruise seasons (April–June, September–November).
  • Check Shuttle Services: Some hotels offer free or low-cost transfers to the pier—confirm when booking.
  • Explore Yichang: Don’t miss the Three Gorges Dam or a stroll along the Binjiang Park riverside promenade.

Whether you’re splurging on luxury or saving for shore excursions, Yichang’s hotel scene has something for every Yangtze cruiser. Pack your bags and get ready for an adventure—your river voyage starts here!
