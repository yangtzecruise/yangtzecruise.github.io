10 Best Yangtze Cruise Tours for an Unforgettable Experience

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

The Yangtze River, China’s longest and the world’s third-longest river, offers some of the most breathtaking landscapes and cultural experiences in the world. A Yangtze River cruise is more than just a journey—it’s an immersion into history, nature, and luxury. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural enrichment, these 10 best Yangtze cruise tours will ensure an unforgettable experience.

1. Luxury All-Inclusive: Victoria Cruises’ Signature Journey

Why Choose This Cruise?

Victoria Cruises is synonymous with luxury on the Yangtze. Their signature journey from Chongqing to Yichang (or vice versa) includes stops at the Three Gorges Dam, Shennong Stream, and Shibaozhai Pagoda.

Highlights:

  • Spacious suites with private balconies
  • Gourmet dining featuring Chinese and Western cuisine
  • Onboard cultural performances and Tai Chi sessions

2. Adventure & Exploration: Century Cruises’ Three Gorges Expedition

Why Choose This Cruise?

For travelers who crave adventure, Century Cruises offers an expedition-style journey through the dramatic Three Gorges—Qutang, Wu, and Xiling.

Highlights:

  • Small-group shore excursions to hidden villages
  • Expert-led lectures on Yangtze River ecology
  • Optional hiking and kayaking activities

3. Family-Friendly Fun: Yangtze Gold Series Cruises

Why Choose This Cruise?

The Yangtze Gold series is perfect for families, offering kid-friendly amenities and activities while still providing a luxurious experience for adults.

Highlights:

  • Dedicated children’s play area and entertainment
  • Interactive cultural workshops (calligraphy, dumpling-making)
  • Spacious family suites with connecting rooms

4. Romantic Getaway: Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer

Why Choose This Cruise?

If you’re looking for an intimate, high-end experience, the Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer is the ultimate romantic escape.

Highlights:

  • Ultra-luxury cabins with butler service
  • Private dining under the stars
  • Couples’ spa treatments with river views

5. Cultural Immersion: Viking Cruises’ China Discovery

Why Choose This Cruise?

Viking Cruises combines a Yangtze River journey with land tours to Beijing, Xi’an, and Shanghai, making it ideal for first-time visitors to China.

Highlights:

  • Guided tours of the Terracotta Warriors and Great Wall
  • Onboard Mandarin language lessons
  • Authentic regional cuisine tastings

6. Budget-Friendly Option: President Cruises’ Classic Itinerary

Why Choose This Cruise?

President Cruises offers an affordable yet comfortable way to experience the Yangtze without sacrificing quality.

Highlights:

  • Well-appointed cabins at a great value
  • Complimentary tea ceremonies
  • Scenic viewing decks for photography

7. Eco-Conscious Travel: Yangtze River Environmental & Cultural Cruise

Why Choose This Cruise?

For eco-tourists, this cruise focuses on sustainability and environmental education while exploring the Yangtze’s natural wonders.

Highlights:

  • Visits to conservation projects along the river
  • Lectures on local wildlife and ecosystems
  • Farm-to-table dining with organic ingredients

8. Extended Exploration: Uniworld’s Grand Yangtze & Tibet Tour

Why Choose This Cruise?

Uniworld’s extended itinerary takes you beyond the river, adding a Tibetan plateau adventure to your Yangtze cruise.

Highlights:

  • Lhasa and Potala Palace visit
  • High-altitude train journey
  • Exclusive Tibetan cultural experiences

9. Photography-Focused: National Geographic Yangtze Expedition

Why Choose This Cruise?

Partnering with National Geographic, this cruise is designed for photography enthusiasts who want to capture the Yangtze’s most stunning vistas.

Highlights:

  • Workshops with professional photographers
  • Sunrise and sunset photo excursions
  • Access to remote scenic spots

10. Festival Special: Yangtze New Year Celebration Cruise

Why Choose This Cruise?

Experience Chinese New Year like never before with themed decorations, traditional performances, and festive dining.

Highlights:

  • Lion dances and fireworks on deck
  • Special holiday menus
  • Red envelope giveaways

Each of these Yangtze cruise tours offers something unique, whether it’s luxury, adventure, culture, or affordability. No matter which one you choose, you’re guaranteed an unforgettable journey along one of the world’s most legendary rivers.
