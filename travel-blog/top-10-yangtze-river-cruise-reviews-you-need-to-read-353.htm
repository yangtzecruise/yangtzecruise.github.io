Top 10 Yangtze River Cruise Reviews You Need to Read

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

The Yangtze River, China’s longest and most iconic waterway, offers one of the world’s most breathtaking cruise experiences. From the towering Three Gorges to the modern marvel of the Three Gorges Dam, a Yangtze River cruise is a journey through history, culture, and stunning landscapes. Whether you’re a luxury traveler, an adventure seeker, or a culture enthusiast, there’s a cruise for you. Here are the top 10 Yangtze River cruise reviews you need to read before booking your trip.

1. Victoria Cruises – The Gold Standard

Why It’s a Must-Read

Victoria Cruises is often hailed as the gold standard for Yangtze River voyages. With decades of experience, their fleet combines Western comforts with authentic Chinese hospitality.

Highlights

  • Luxury Cabins: Spacious rooms with private balconies for uninterrupted views.
  • Cultural Immersion: Onboard performances like Sichuan opera and calligraphy demonstrations.
  • Gourmet Dining: A mix of Chinese and Western cuisine, with fresh local ingredients.

What Reviewers Say

Travelers rave about the impeccable service and well-curated shore excursions, including visits to the Shibaozhai Pagoda and the Lesser Three Gorges.

2. Century Cruises – Modern Elegance

Why It’s a Must-Read

Century Cruises stands out for its sleek, modern ships and eco-friendly initiatives. Their "Paragon" and "Legend" ships are among the newest on the river.

Highlights

  • High-Tech Amenities: In-room tablets for itinerary updates and on-demand entertainment.
  • Sustainability: Advanced wastewater treatment systems to protect the Yangtze’s ecosystem.
  • Panoramic Lounges: Floor-to-ceiling windows for 360-degree views.

What Reviewers Say

Guests love the spacious sundecks and the emphasis on sustainability, making it a top pick for environmentally conscious travelers.

3. President Cruises – Affordable Luxury

Why It’s a Must-Read

President Cruises strikes a perfect balance between affordability and luxury, making it ideal for mid-range budgets.

Highlights

  • Value for Money: Competitive pricing without sacrificing quality.
  • Local Flavors: Authentic Chinese banquets with regional specialties.
  • Friendly Staff: Multilingual crew members who go above and beyond.

What Reviewers Say

Many highlight the warm service and the well-organized excursions, particularly the Fengdu Ghost City tour.

4. Yangzi Explorer – Boutique Luxury

Why It’s a Must-Read

For those seeking exclusivity, Yangzi Explorer offers a boutique experience with only 62 suites.

Highlights

  • Intimate Setting: A smaller ship means personalized attention.
  • Butler Service: Dedicated butlers for each suite.
  • Helicopter Tours: Optional add-ons for a bird’s-eye view of the gorges.

What Reviewers Say

The helicopter tour over the Three Gorges is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, according to many guests.

5. Viking Cruises – Western Comforts

Why It’s a Must-Read

Viking brings its renowned European river cruise expertise to the Yangtze, catering to Western travelers.

Highlights

  • Familiar Comforts: Western-style beds, English-speaking guides, and familiar cuisine.
  • Comprehensive Packages: Often bundled with land tours to Beijing or Shanghai.
  • Lectures & Workshops: Enrichment programs about Chinese history and culture.

What Reviewers Say

Western travelers appreciate the seamless experience and the ease of communication.

6. Sanxia Cruise – Budget-Friendly Option

Why It’s a Must-Read

Sanxia Cruise is perfect for budget-conscious travelers who still want a memorable experience.

Highlights

  • Affordable Rates: Great for backpackers or families.
  • Local Charm: Less commercialized, with a focus on authentic interactions.
  • Short Itineraries: Ideal for those with limited time.

What Reviewers Say

Guests enjoy the no-frills approach and the chance to mingle with local passengers.

7. Regal Cruises – Family-Friendly Fun

Why It’s a Must-Read

Regal Cruises caters to families with kid-friendly amenities and activities.

Highlights

  • Kids’ Clubs: Supervised activities to keep children entertained.
  • Interconnecting Cabins: Perfect for families.
  • Varied Excursions: Options for all age groups.

What Reviewers Say

Parents love the stress-free environment and the variety of onboard entertainment.

8. Oriental Royal Cruises – Cultural Depth

Why It’s a Must-Read

Oriental Royal Cruises emphasizes cultural immersion, with expert-led tours and workshops.

Highlights

  • Scholar-Led Talks: In-depth discussions on Chinese history.
  • Hands-On Activities: Calligraphy, tea ceremonies, and more.
  • Traditional Decor: Ships designed with classic Chinese aesthetics.

What Reviewers Say

Culture buffs appreciate the depth of the programming and the knowledgeable guides.

9. New Century Cruises – Tech-Savvy Travel

Why It’s a Must-Read

New Century Cruises appeals to tech-savvy travelers with cutting-edge amenities.

Highlights

  • Virtual Reality Tours: Preview excursions before you go.
  • Smart Rooms: Voice-controlled lighting and temperature.
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi: Stay connected even on the river.

What Reviewers Say

Tech enthusiasts love the innovative features and the seamless connectivity.

10. Golden Era Cruises – Romantic Getaway

Why It’s a Must-Read

Golden Era Cruises is designed for couples, with romantic packages and private dining options.

Highlights

  • Honeymoon Suites: Lavish accommodations with champagne on arrival.
  • Sunset Deck Dinners: Private meals under the stars.
  • Couples’ Spa Treatments: Relaxing massages for two.

What Reviewers Say

Couples rave about the intimate atmosphere and the attention to detail.

Each of these cruises offers a unique way to experience the Yangtze River. Whether you prioritize luxury, culture, family fun, or romance, there’s a perfect voyage waiting for you.
